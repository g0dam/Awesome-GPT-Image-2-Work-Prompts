# Packaging dieline supplier mockup

**Target user:** product manager

**Use case:** Prepare a packaging handoff board for supplier review

## Prompt

Create a 4:3 business-ready image for a product manager. Goal: Prepare a packaging handoff board for supplier review. Deliverable: Packaging dieline supplier mockup. Visual direction: An unfolded box dieline with panels, inserts, material notes, label placements, barcode placeholder, finish callouts and a small assembled 3D preview. Layout and content requirements: show print-safe zones, fold lines and panel labels; include material, finish and insert notes as readable callouts; use a small assembled package preview next to the flat dieline; avoid real certification marks or manufacturer logos; make the asset useful for supplier discussion. Use precise layout language, professional work context, crisp readable labels, realistic interface or document details where useful, and enough negative space for website cards and GitHub preview crops. Avoid real brand marks, fake certification logos, entertainment packaging, unreadable annotations, distorted dieline geometry, messy mockup clutter.
