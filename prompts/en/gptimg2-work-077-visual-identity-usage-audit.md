# Visual identity usage audit

**Target user** brand designer

**Use case** Review brand consistency across major touchpoints and define remediation priorities

## Prompt

Create a 4:3 business-ready image for a brand designer. Goal: Review brand consistency across major touchpoints and define remediation priorities. Deliverable: Visual identity usage audit. Visual direction: A brand governance audit grid with website header, email banner, social card, product UI, sales slide and event signage examples marked by pass/fail criteria. Use precise visual design language: define focal point, grid, hierarchy, typography, color role, spacing, annotations, crop safety, and realistic work context. Keep labels short and readable; make the artifact useful for a real professional review, campaign, handoff, or operating meeting. Avoid real brand names, copied logos, celebrity likenesses, private data, fake guarantees, unreadable microtext, clutter, distorted UI, generic stock-photo people, and purely decorative entertainment styling.
