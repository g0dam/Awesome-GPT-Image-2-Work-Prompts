## Prompt

Create a 信任导向广告，含简单工作流图、轻微紧迫感标签，不使用虚假折扣或操控性文案。 business-ready image for a growth marketer. Goal: Create a retargeting ad for visitors who abandoned a trial signup. Use precise layout language, keep text short and legible, show realistic work context, include enough negative space for website cropping, avoid generic stock-photo smiles, fantasy elements, celebrity likenesses, copyrighted brands, unreadable text, clutter, warped hands, and exaggerated claims.
