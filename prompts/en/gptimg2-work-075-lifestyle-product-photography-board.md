# Lifestyle product photography art-direction board

**Target user** ecommerce creative director

**Use case** Plan a product photography shot list and production direction for a listing campaign

## Prompt

Create a 16:9 business-ready image for a ecommerce creative director. Goal: Plan a product photography shot list and production direction for a listing campaign. Deliverable: Lifestyle product photography art-direction board. Visual direction: A refined production moodboard with six shot frames, lighting diagram, prop list, surface materials, crop ratios, color palette and retouching notes. Use precise visual design language: define focal point, grid, hierarchy, typography, color role, spacing, annotations, crop safety, and realistic work context. Keep labels short and readable; make the artifact useful for a real professional review, campaign, handoff, or operating meeting. Avoid real brand names, copied logos, celebrity likenesses, private data, fake guarantees, unreadable microtext, clutter, distorted UI, generic stock-photo people, and purely decorative entertainment styling.
