**Target user** service designer

## Prompt

Create a 16:9 business-ready image for a service designer. Goal: Prepare a structured strategy workshop board for facilitation and synthesis. Deliverable: Workshop facilitation wallboard. Visual direction: A workshop board with agenda, breakout activities, dot voting, decision log, parking lot, stakeholder map, synthesis canvas and facilitator notes. Use precise visual design language: define focal point, grid, hierarchy, typography, color role, spacing, annotations, crop safety, and realistic work context. Keep labels short and readable; make the artifact useful for a real professional review, campaign, handoff, or operating meeting. Avoid real brand names, copied logos, celebrity likenesses, private data, fake guarantees, unreadable microtext, clutter, distorted UI, generic stock-photo people, and purely decorative entertainment styling.
