# Executive pitch deck storyboard

**Target user** sales designer

**Use case** Plan a sales deck narrative before creating high-fidelity slides

## Prompt

Create a 4:3 business-ready image for a sales designer. Goal: Plan a sales deck narrative before creating high-fidelity slides. Deliverable: Executive pitch deck storyboard. Visual direction: A 10-slide pitch sequence with slide roles, visual rhythm notes, audience emotion curve, evidence markers and recommended chart types. Use precise visual design language: define focal point, grid, hierarchy, typography, color role, spacing, annotations, crop safety, and realistic work context. Keep labels short and readable; make the artifact useful for a real professional review, campaign, handoff, or operating meeting. Avoid real brand names, copied logos, celebrity likenesses, private data, fake guarantees, unreadable microtext, clutter, distorted UI, generic stock-photo people, and purely decorative entertainment styling.
