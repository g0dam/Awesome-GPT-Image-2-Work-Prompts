# Procurement business case visual

**Target user:** sales lead

**Use case:** Create an enterprise buying-committee business case for software procurement

## Prompt

Create a 16:9 business-ready image for a sales lead. Goal: Create an enterprise buying-committee business case for software procurement. Deliverable: Procurement business case visual. Visual direction: A one-page decision board with problem cost, risk reduction, implementation timeline, stakeholder approvals, ROI range and a final decision box. Layout and content requirements: make the flow understandable to finance and procurement stakeholders; show evidence blocks, timeline and approval path; use cautious language and placeholder metrics; include risk and implementation detail without overloading the page; make the visual feel enterprise-ready. Use precise layout language, professional work context, crisp readable labels, realistic interface or document details where useful, and enough negative space for website cards and GitHub preview crops. Avoid aggressive sales copy, guaranteed ROI, fake real company names, real logos, stock-photo executives, dense tiny numbers, clutter.
