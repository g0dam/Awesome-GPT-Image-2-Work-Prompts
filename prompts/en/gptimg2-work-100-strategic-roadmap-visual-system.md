# Strategic roadmap visual system

**Target user** founder

**Use case** Plan a four-quarter roadmap with themes, bets, milestones and decision gates

## Prompt

Create a 16:9 business-ready image for a founder. Goal: Plan a four-quarter roadmap with themes, bets, milestones and decision gates. Deliverable: Strategic roadmap visual system. Visual direction: A four-quarter executive roadmap with themes, strategic bets, milestones, resource allocation, evidence, dependencies, risks and decision gates. Use precise visual design language: define focal point, grid, hierarchy, typography, color role, spacing, annotations, crop safety, and realistic work context. Keep labels short and readable; make the artifact useful for a real professional review, campaign, handoff, or operating meeting. Avoid real brand names, copied logos, celebrity likenesses, private data, fake guarantees, unreadable microtext, clutter, distorted UI, generic stock-photo people, and purely decorative entertainment styling.
