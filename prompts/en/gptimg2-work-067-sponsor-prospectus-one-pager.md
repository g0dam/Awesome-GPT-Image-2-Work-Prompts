# Sponsor prospectus one-pager

**Target user:** events marketer

**Use case:** Pitch sponsors for a professional B2B conference or industry event

## Prompt

Create a 16:9 business-ready image for a events marketer. Goal: Pitch sponsors for a professional B2B conference or industry event. Deliverable: Sponsor prospectus one-pager. Visual direction: A credible event sales sheet with audience profile, sponsorship tiers, booth visibility map, content opportunities, lead capture benefits, brand safety notes and contact area. Layout and content requirements: use placeholder audience and sponsor data only; show tiers, visibility and content opportunities clearly; include brand safety and next-step sections; feel premium but not festival-like; avoid fake inflated numbers. Use precise layout language, professional work context, crisp readable labels, realistic interface or document details where useful, and enough negative space for website cards and GitHub preview crops. Avoid real sponsor logos, fake attendee photos, inflated audience numbers, tiny text, clutter, party poster styling.
