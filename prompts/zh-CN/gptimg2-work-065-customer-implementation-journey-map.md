# 客户实施旅程图

**使用场景：** 为中型客户实施项目创建上线旅程图

## Prompt

为客户成功经理生成一张 16:9 的真实工作场景图片。目标：为中型客户实施项目创建上线旅程图。交付物：客户实施旅程图。视觉方向：A customer success playbook map from kickoff, data setup, configuration, training, pilot, go-live and adoption review.。版式与内容要求：show vendor and customer responsibilities in separate lanes；include risk flags, success criteria and meeting cadence；add artifact checklist and milestone markers；use clear swimlanes and concise labels；keep it realistic for onboarding discussions。请使用明确的版式语言、专业工作语境、清晰可读的短标签、必要的界面或文档细节，并保留适合网站卡片和 GitHub 预览裁切的留白。避免：real company names, vague smiley customer photos, unrealistic promises, clutter, tiny text, decorative-only journey maps。
