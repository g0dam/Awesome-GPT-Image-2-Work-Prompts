**目标用户：** 支持运营经理

**使用场景：** 创建用于监控 SLA 风险和排班动作的支持运营大屏

## Prompt

为支持运营经理生成一张 16:9 的真实工作场景图片。目标：创建用于监控 SLA 风险和排班动作的支持运营大屏。交付物：SLA 监控大屏。视觉方向：A command-center dashboard with support queues, response-time gauges, escalation lanes, regional workload distribution, backlog aging and staffing suggestions.。版式与内容要求：show compact dashboard modules that could work on an office screen；use clear status colors and readable high-contrast labels；include alert panel and suggested staffing actions；keep metrics realistic and neutral；avoid sci-fi display tropes。请使用明确的版式语言、专业工作语境、清晰可读的短标签、必要的界面或文档细节，并保留适合网站卡片和 GitHub 预览裁切的留白。避免：excessive glow, sci-fi fantasy UI, fake brand names, unrealistic metrics, unreadable charts, cluttered dashboards。
