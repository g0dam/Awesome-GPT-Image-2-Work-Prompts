# 仓储流程模拟故事板

**目标用户：** 培训设计师

**使用场景：** 通过情境分镜讲解新的仓库收货流程

## Prompt

为培训设计师生成一张 16:9 的真实工作场景图片。目标：通过情境分镜讲解新的仓库收货流程。交付物：仓储流程模拟故事板。视觉方向：A six-panel training storyboard from delivery arrival to inspection, scanning, exception routing, inventory update and supervisor sign-off.。版式与内容要求：show six sequential panels with role badges and decision points；include timing markers, exception routing and safety reminders；use realistic warehouse context without unsafe behavior；make arrows and process order clear；keep text short and training-friendly。请使用明确的版式语言、专业工作语境、清晰可读的短标签、必要的界面或文档细节，并保留适合网站卡片和 GitHub 预览裁切的留白。避免：comic entertainment styling, unsafe warehouse practices, real company logos, excessive text, distorted equipment, cluttered panels。
