# 销售异议处理战卡

**目标用户：** 客户经理

**使用场景：** 为处理买方异议创建实用销售赋能卡片

## Prompt

为客户经理生成一张 4:3 的真实工作场景图片。目标：为处理买方异议创建实用销售赋能卡片。交付物：销售异议处理战卡。视觉方向：A compact enablement card with objection categories, response framing, proof asset placeholders, competitor-neutral comparison cues, discovery questions and next-step checklist.。版式与内容要求：cover budget, timing, need and competition objections；separate response framing from proof assets and questions；keep tone consultative rather than manipulative；use short bullets and clear sections；avoid real competitor names。请使用明确的版式语言、专业工作语境、清晰可读的短标签、必要的界面或文档细节，并保留适合网站卡片和 GitHub 预览裁切的留白。避免：real competitor names, inflated claims, manipulative fear tactics, stock-photo people, tiny text, cluttered battlecard grids。
